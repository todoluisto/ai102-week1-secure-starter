Technical Lead — Enterprise Data Management

Major Healthcare Organization (Confidential)
Location: Northeast US (Hybrid)
Compensation: $155,000 - $190,000

- Produce technical documentation and status reporting

Desired Background:
- 8+ years in data management or engineering roles
- Experience in healthcare or regulated industries
- Familiarity with enterprise data tools (Informatica, SSIS, Azure, others)
- SQL and general programming ability
- Understanding of data governance frameworks
- Experience managing vendors and contractor teams
- PMP, DAMA, or similar certifications are a plus
