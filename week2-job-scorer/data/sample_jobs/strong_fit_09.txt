Senior Data Engineer - Data Mesh Implementation

Location: Chicago, IL (Remote OK)
Compensation: Competitive base + bonus + equity

About the Company:
Fortune 500 retail company undergoing massive digital transformation. We're moving from a centralized data warehouse to a federated data mesh architecture and need experienced engineers to drive this initiative.

The Role:
As a Senior Data Engineer, you will be a key contributor to our data mesh implementation, working with domain teams to build self-serve data products. This is a greenfield opportunity to shape the future of data at a company with 100,000+ employees.

Responsibilities:
- Design and implement data product architectures aligned with data mesh principles
- Build scalable pipelines using Azure Databricks, Data Factory, and Python
- Create standardized templates and patterns for domain teams to follow
- Implement data contracts and quality SLAs between producer and consumer teams
- Develop self-serve tooling on top of Databricks Unity Catalog
- Contribute to our internal data platform as a product

Qualifications:
- 8+ years in data engineering roles
- Strong Python and SQL expertise
- Production experience with Azure Databricks and the broader Azure data stack
- Understanding of data mesh, data products, and domain-driven design
- Experience building reusable frameworks and developer tooling
- Excellent written and verbal communication

Preferred:
- Multi-cloud (Azure + GCP) experience
- Terraform, Azure DevOps, or GitHub Actions
- Experience in retail, supply chain, or logistics

Benefits: medical/dental/vision, pension, tuition reimbursement, employee discount.
