Director of Data Engineering — Databricks

San Francisco, CA (Hybrid)
Total Comp: $350,000 - $500,000

Databricks is hiring a Director of Data Engineering to lead our internal data platform team. Yes, we use our own product — and we need a leader to ensure we're the best example of what a modern data organization looks like.

About the Role:
You'll lead a team of 15+ data engineers who build and maintain the internal data infrastructure that powers Databricks' business operations, product analytics, and go-to-market functions. This is a senior leadership role reporting to the VP of Engineering.

Responsibilities:
- Lead and grow a team of 15+ data engineers across 3 sub-teams
- Define the strategic vision for internal data infrastructure
- Drive adoption of lakehouse architecture using Databricks products
- Partner with Product, Sales, Finance, and Marketing leadership
- Manage $5M+ annual cloud infrastructure budget
- Represent the "customer zero" perspective to product teams
- Build a culture of engineering excellence and innovation

Requirements:
- 12+ years in data engineering, 5+ years in engineering management
- Track record of building and scaling data teams (10+ engineers)
- Deep technical expertise in data platforms and architectures
- Experience with Databricks (strongly preferred — we eat our own dogfood)
- Strategic thinker with executive presence
- P&L or budget management experience
- MS in Computer Science or equivalent

This is a Director-level role. While the tech stack will feel familiar, the scope is primarily leadership and strategy. Candidates looking for a hands-on IC role should look at our Staff/Principal openings instead.
