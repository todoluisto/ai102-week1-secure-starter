Engineering Manager, Data Platform

Location: Denver, CO (Hybrid) or Remote
Level: M1 (first-line manager)
Compensation: $190K-$230K base + bonus + RSUs

Who We Are:
We're a public cloud security company (4,000+ employees) protecting enterprises from cyber threats. Data is at the core of everything we do — our products analyze billions of security events daily.

The Role:
We're looking for an Engineering Manager to lead our Data Platform team (7 engineers). You'll be responsible for both the technical direction and the people on the team.

What You'll Do:
- Manage a team of 7 data engineers across multiple time zones
- Drive technical roadmap for our Azure-based data platform
- Partner with product, security research, and ML teams on priorities
- Own team hiring, performance reviews, and career development
- Remove blockers and ensure the team ships reliably
- Stay technically engaged — code reviews, architecture discussions, design docs
- Manage budget and cloud cost optimization

What We Need:
- 8+ years in data/software engineering, 2+ years in management
- Strong technical background in data platforms (Azure, Databricks, Spark)
- Python and SQL proficiency (you should be able to review PRs meaningfully)
- Experience running agile teams and managing delivery
- Track record of growing engineers and building inclusive teams
- Excellent communication and stakeholder management

Preferred:
- GCP or multi-cloud experience
- Security or cybersecurity domain knowledge
- Experience scaling teams during rapid growth

We know management is hard. We invest heavily in manager development, executive coaching, and peer learning circles.
