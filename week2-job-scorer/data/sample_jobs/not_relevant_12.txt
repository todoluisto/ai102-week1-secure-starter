Salesforce Administrator / Developer

Location: Chicago, IL (Hybrid — 2 days onsite)
Salary: $95,000 - $125,000

We're a B2B SaaS company looking for a Salesforce Admin/Developer to own our Salesforce instance and support our growing sales and customer success teams.

Responsibilities:
- Administer and customize our Salesforce Sales Cloud and Service Cloud instances
- Build custom objects, fields, validation rules, and page layouts
- Develop Apex triggers, classes, and Visualforce pages
- Create and maintain Salesforce reports and dashboards
- Manage user access, profiles, permission sets, and roles
- Implement and manage Salesforce integrations (HubSpot, Zendesk, NetSuite)
- Build automated workflows using Flow Builder
- Support data migration and deduplication efforts
- Train internal users on Salesforce features and best practices

Requirements:
- 4+ years of Salesforce administration experience
- Salesforce Admin (ADM-201) certification required
- Platform Developer I certification preferred
- Experience with Apex, SOQL, and Lightning Web Components
- Understanding of Salesforce data model and security model
- Experience with Salesforce integrations (REST/SOAP APIs)
- Strong communication and stakeholder management skills

Nice to Have:
- Salesforce CPQ experience
- Pardot or Marketing Cloud experience
- Experience with MuleSoft or Workato for integrations
- Additional Salesforce certifications

Our CRM is the backbone of our go-to-market operations. You'll work closely with sales ops, marketing ops, and customer success to ensure Salesforce supports our growth. This is a CRM role, not a data engineering position.
