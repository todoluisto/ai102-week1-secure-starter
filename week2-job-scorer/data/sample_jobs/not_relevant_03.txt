Junior Data Analyst

Location: Atlanta, GA (Hybrid — 3 days onsite)
Salary: $55,000 - $70,000

We're hiring a Junior Data Analyst to join our business intelligence team. This is an entry-level role perfect for recent graduates or career changers looking to break into data analytics.

What You'll Do:
- Create and maintain reports and dashboards in Tableau
- Write basic SQL queries to extract data for analysis
- Clean and prepare data in Excel and Google Sheets
- Support monthly and quarterly business reporting
- Assist senior analysts with ad hoc data requests
- Document data definitions and report methodologies

Requirements:
- Bachelor's degree in Business, Statistics, Economics, or related field
- 0-2 years of experience in data analysis or related work
- Basic SQL knowledge (SELECT, JOIN, GROUP BY)
- Proficiency in Excel (pivot tables, VLOOKUP, basic formulas)
- Experience with at least one BI tool (Tableau, Power BI, or Looker)
- Strong attention to detail and organizational skills
- Good written and verbal communication

Nice to Have:
- Internship experience in data analysis
- Basic Python or R knowledge
- Statistics coursework
- Experience with Google Analytics

This is a great opportunity to start your career in data. We provide mentorship, training budget, and a clear path to Analyst II within 18-24 months. We value curiosity and a willingness to learn over years of experience.

Benefits: health insurance, 15 days PTO, 401k, hybrid work arrangement.
