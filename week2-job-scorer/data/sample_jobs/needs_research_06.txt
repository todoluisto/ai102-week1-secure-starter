Data Solutions Architect

Global Systems Integrator
Anywhere in the US | Up to 50% travel
Base: $160K-$200K + utilization bonus

We help enterprises modernize their data infrastructure. As a Data Solutions Architect, you'll work with clients across industries to design and deliver data platform solutions.

Honestly, the day-to-day varies a lot. Some weeks you're designing architecture diagrams. Other weeks you're writing code. Some months you're on a long-term engagement, other months you're bouncing between pre-sales activities.

Typical Engagements:
- Cloud data platform migration (on-prem to Azure/GCP/AWS)
- Data lake/lakehouse design and implementation
- Data governance and quality assessments
- Analytics platform modernization
- Data strategy roadmap development

Skills We Value:
- Data engineering and architecture background
- Multi-cloud experience (Azure, GCP, AWS — the more the better)
- Databricks, Snowflake, or similar platforms
- Python, SQL, Spark
- Ability to translate technical concepts for business audiences
- Comfort with client-facing work and travel

What's Unclear:
- Specific projects depend on what's sold and staffed
- Your team varies by engagement
- Work-life balance depends heavily on the project
- Utilization targets drive a significant portion of compensation
- Bench time between projects is common and can feel unproductive

Certifications are valued. We'll pay for Azure, GCP, Databricks, and Snowflake certs. There's a clear promotion path but it's tied to business development contribution.
