Data Engineer — SpaceX Starlink

Hawthorne, CA (Onsite Only — No Remote)
Compensation: $140,000 - $175,000

SpaceX is looking for a Data Engineer to support the Starlink constellation operations team. You'll build data systems that monitor and optimize the performance of 5,000+ satellites in orbit.

Responsibilities:
- Build data pipelines for satellite telemetry data processing
- Develop real-time monitoring dashboards for constellation health
- Create analytics infrastructure for network performance optimization
- Process and analyze orbital mechanics data for mission planning
- Collaborate with firmware, network, and operations teams

Requirements:
- 3-6 years of data engineering experience
- Python and SQL proficiency
- Experience with cloud data platforms (GCP preferred)
- Understanding of time-series data and real-time processing
- Strong problem-solving skills and ability to work in fast-paced environment
- US Person status required (ITAR compliance)

Nice to Have:
- Aerospace or telecommunications experience
- Experience with Kubernetes and containerized workloads
- Knowledge of Apache Kafka or similar streaming platforms
- Familiarity with Databricks or Spark

This is a mid-level role. We expect you to grow quickly given the unique challenges. Our engineers work hard — expect 50+ hour weeks during critical mission phases.

We offer medical/dental/vision, stock options, and the chance to work on technology that connects the unconnected. Onsite work in Hawthorne, CA is required — no exceptions.
