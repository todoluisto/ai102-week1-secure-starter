Data Platform Lead

Remote (US) | Reports to VP of Engineering
Base: $195K-$235K + equity

We're a growth-stage B2B SaaS company (400 employees, $150M ARR) looking for a Data Platform Lead to build and manage our data engineering function from the ground up.

This is a player-coach role. You'll spend about 60% of your time on hands-on engineering and 40% on team leadership, hiring, and strategy.

What You'll Build:
- A world-class data platform on Azure and Databricks
- A team of 4-6 data engineers over the next 12 months
- Data infrastructure supporting product analytics, customer success, and finance
- Self-serve analytics capabilities for 200+ internal users
- Data governance framework and data catalog

What We Need:
- 8+ years of data engineering experience
- 2+ years of people management or tech lead experience
- Deep technical skills in Python, SQL, and cloud data platforms
- Azure and/or GCP experience (we're primarily Azure)
- Experience building data teams and defining hiring bars
- Strong opinions on data architecture, loosely held
- Ability to translate business needs into technical roadmaps

Technical Stack:
Azure Databricks, ADLS Gen2, Azure Data Factory, dbt, Airflow, Terraform, Python, SQL

The Catch:
This is as much a leadership role as a technical one. You need to be comfortable context-switching between writing Spark code and running 1:1s with your reports. If you've been a senior IC wanting to move into leadership, this could be your shot.
