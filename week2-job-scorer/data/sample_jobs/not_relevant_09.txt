UX/UI Designer — Enterprise SaaS

Location: Remote (US/EU)
Salary: $120,000 - $155,000

We're hiring a UX/UI Designer to redesign our enterprise analytics dashboard. You'll work closely with product managers and engineers to create intuitive experiences for complex data workflows.

Responsibilities:
- Lead end-to-end design for our analytics platform UI
- Conduct user research interviews and usability testing
- Create wireframes, prototypes, and high-fidelity mockups in Figma
- Build and maintain our design system and component library
- Collaborate with frontend engineers on implementation
- Define interaction patterns for data visualization components
- Present design decisions to stakeholders and leadership

Requirements:
- 5+ years of UX/UI design experience
- Strong portfolio demonstrating enterprise SaaS or dashboard design
- Expert Figma skills (auto-layout, components, variables)
- Experience with design systems and component libraries
- User research skills (interviews, surveys, usability testing)
- Understanding of data visualization principles
- Ability to simplify complex workflows into intuitive interfaces

Nice to Have:
- Experience designing for data-heavy applications
- Familiarity with accessibility standards (WCAG)
- Basic HTML/CSS understanding
- Experience with analytics or BI products
- Motion design skills (Principle, After Effects)

We use Figma, FigJam, Maze, and Loom as our core design tools. You'll work in a cross-functional squad with a PM, 2 frontend engineers, and a QA engineer. Design has a seat at the table here.
