Associate Data Engineer — Meta (Threads Team)

Menlo Park, CA or New York, NY (Hybrid — 3 days onsite)
Salary Range: $125,000 - $165,000

The Threads team at Meta is growing fast and we need data engineers to build the analytics infrastructure for one of the fastest-growing social platforms in history.

About Threads:
Threads launched in 2023 and has grown to 200M+ monthly active users. We're in a unique position — building a new product with the scale and resources of Meta behind us.

The Role:
As an Associate Data Engineer, you'll work on the data pipelines that power product analytics, content recommendations, and integrity systems for Threads.

What You'll Do:
- Build data pipelines using Spark and internal Meta tools
- Process billions of user interaction events daily
- Develop data models for product analytics and experimentation
- Support A/B testing infrastructure
- Create dashboards and automated reporting

Requirements:
- 2-4 years of data engineering experience
- Python and SQL proficiency
- Experience with distributed data processing (Spark, Hive, Presto)
- Understanding of data modeling concepts
- Strong analytical and problem-solving skills
- BS in Computer Science or related field

Preferred:
- Experience with large-scale consumer products
- Knowledge of recommendation systems
- Familiarity with privacy-preserving analytics
- Experience with workflow orchestration tools

Note: This is an E4 (Associate) level position. Senior candidates will be considered for E5 (Mid-level) if they have 5+ years of relevant experience. The role is hybrid — onsite presence in either Menlo Park or NYC is required.
