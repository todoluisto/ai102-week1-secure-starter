Senior Data Engineer II

Department: Data Platform
Location: Remote (US/Canada)

- Proficiency in Python, PySpark, and SQL
- Experience with Azure services: Databricks, Data Factory, Event Hubs, ADLS
- Solid understanding of data warehousing and lakehouse patterns
- Familiarity with version control, testing frameworks, and agile practices

Preferred Qualifications:
- GCP experience (BigQuery, Dataflow)
- Insurance or financial services domain knowledge
- dbt, Great Expectations, or similar tools
- Apache Kafka or similar streaming platforms
