Product Marketing Manager — Developer Tools

Location: San Francisco, CA (Hybrid)
Compensation: $145,000 - $180,000 + bonus

We're a developer tools company and we need a Product Marketing Manager to help us tell the story of our data platform to engineering audiences.

The Role:
You'll own the go-to-market strategy for our data engineering product line. This means positioning, messaging, competitive analysis, and sales enablement — all aimed at technical buyers.

Responsibilities:
- Develop product positioning and messaging for data engineering personas
- Create technical content (blog posts, whitepapers, case studies)
- Plan and execute product launches
- Build competitive intelligence and battle cards
- Enable sales team with technical talking points and demos
- Manage analyst relations (Gartner, Forrester)
- Organize and speak at industry events and conferences
- Track market trends in data engineering and cloud infrastructure

Requirements:
- 5+ years of product marketing experience in B2B tech
- Experience marketing to technical audiences (developers, data engineers)
- Understanding of the data engineering ecosystem (Databricks, Snowflake, etc.)
- Excellent writing and presentation skills
- Data-driven approach to measuring marketing effectiveness
- Experience with product launches and go-to-market planning
- BA/BS required, MBA preferred

Nice to Have:
- Previous engineering or technical background
- Experience at a data or analytics company
- Conference speaking experience
- Familiarity with developer community marketing

This is a marketing role, not an engineering role. You should understand data engineering concepts but you won't be writing code.
