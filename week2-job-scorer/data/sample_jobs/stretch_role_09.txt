Senior Data Architect

Global Consulting Firm — Technology Practice
Multiple US Locations + Remote Options

- Build proof-of-concept implementations using Databricks, Synapse, or BigQuery
- Create architectural decision records, reference architectures, and best practices
- Mentor consulting teams and contribute to thought leadership
- Support pre-sales activities and proposal development

Qualifications:
- 10+ years in data engineering or data architecture
- Deep expertise in at least one cloud platform (Azure preferred)
- Strong skills in Python, SQL, and data modeling
- Experience with Databricks, Snowflake, or similar platforms
- Consulting experience or strong client-facing skills
- Excellent presentation and written communication
- Understanding of data governance, security, and compliance frameworks

Preferred:
- Multi-cloud experience (Azure + GCP)
- Industry certifications (Azure Data Engineer, Databricks, etc.)
- Published articles or conference presentations
- Experience with data mesh, data fabric, or similar paradigms

Travel required. Relocation assistance available.
