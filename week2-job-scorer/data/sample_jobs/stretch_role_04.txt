Analytics Engineering Manager

Remote First | $175K - $210K base + bonus + equity

About Us
We're a Series D data observability company. Our product helps data teams trust their data by detecting anomalies, tracking lineage, and measuring data quality across the modern data stack.

About the Role
We're hiring an Analytics Engineering Manager to lead our internal analytics engineering team (5 engineers). You'll own the data models, metrics definitions, and analytics infrastructure that drive company decisions.

This is a unique opportunity to apply data engineering skills in a management capacity at a company where data quality is literally the product.

Responsibilities:
- Manage and grow a team of 5 analytics engineers
- Own our internal dbt project and analytics warehouse architecture
- Define company-wide metrics and KPI frameworks
- Build and maintain executive dashboards and self-serve analytics
- Partner with product, sales, and finance on data needs
- Drive adoption of analytics engineering best practices
- Contribute hands-on code when needed (expect ~30% IC work)

Requirements:
- 7+ years in data/analytics engineering
- 1+ years managing a technical team
- Expert SQL and strong Python skills
- Experience with dbt (this is central to the role)
- Cloud data platform experience (Databricks, Snowflake, BigQuery)
- Understanding of data modeling (Kimball, OBT, wide tables)
- Experience with BI tools (Looker, Tableau, or Power BI)

Nice to Have:
- Azure and/or GCP hands-on experience
- Data observability or data quality domain knowledge
- Experience defining metrics layers (MetricFlow, Cube)

We believe diverse teams build better products.
